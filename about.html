Tronixworkshop is a blog about the electronics projects created and built in my home workshop using anything I can get my hands on from 555 timers to microcontrollers from FPGA's to Operational Amplifiers.

Discover how I turned my unused garage space into a respectable electronics laboratory and hacker space, used to create some excellent open source projects and gadgets that I share with you. Learn and understand about the tools, techniques, and craft used to turn wonderful ideas into a reality.
